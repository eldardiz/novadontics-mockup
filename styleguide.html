The mark

One symbol. Three lockups.

The existing Novadontics mark is preserved without modification. Lockups adapt to surface and context.

On Cream

Clear space: maintain a minimum margin equal to the height of the wordmark's lowercase letter on every side. Minimum size: 24 px on screen, 0.5 in in print. Do not stretch, recolor outside this system, or pair with competing graphic elements.

03
Typography

Editorial display. Industrial body.

An Instrument Serif & Inter pairing. Serif for voice and weight. Sans for clarity and structure.

Aa
Instrument Serif
Display · Regular & Italic

Used for headings, prices, large numerical figures, and editorial pull quotes. Italic carries emotional emphasis — apply to one word per headline, no more.

Plans designed for every practice.

UI & body · 400 / 500 / 600

Used for body copy, UI labels, table content, buttons, and microcopy. Set body at 16 px with 1.6 line-height. Eyebrow labels: 12 px, 500 weight, uppercase, tracked +0.14 em.
