04 · Questions
Common questions

Questions, answered.

How is pricing actually determined?

Every practice has a different workflow, patient volume, and specialty mix. We tailor each plan to what you actually need — no padding, no surprise modules, no per-seat creep.

Are there setup fees?

No setup fees on any tier. Onboarding and data migration from your existing platform are included from day one.

How many users does each plan support?

Unlimited users per office on every tier. You'll never pay per seat or have to debate which staff member gets a license this month.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or rebalance at any time. Your tier should match where your practice is today, not where it was a year ago.

Is my patient data HIPAA compliant?

All Novadontics infrastructure is HIPAA compliant, encrypted at rest and in transit, and hosted on US-based servers with continuous audit trails.

Which third-party integrations are supported?

Major imaging vendors, eRx providers, insurance clearinghouses, and payment processors. Custom integrations available for groups and DSOs.

Do you offer training?

Live onboarding sessions, a recorded training library, and ongoing US-based support are included on every plan.

What happens to my existing patient data?

Our migration team handles the move from your current platform end-to-end. You go live with your full patient history intact and verified.
